Funtime Photography specialises in event photography across Sussex, covering everything from local parties and club nights to large festival weekends. Based in Uckfield, we work with promoters, venues, and organisers to capture the real atmosphere of live events.

Our photographers don’t just turn up and shoot from the sidelines. We blend into the crowd, connect with people, and move with the music to capture genuine moments — the reactions, the energy, and the details that matter long after the night is over.

Whether you’re running a one‑off celebration or a recurring event that goes late into the night, our approach is relaxed, professional, and focused on telling the full story of your event through natural, engaging photography.
